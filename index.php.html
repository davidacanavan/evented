Create

Set up an account with us and begin promoting and selling within minutes. A small deposit will be taken to protect the community and ensure the trust of the user-base.

Promote

Promote your event to the extent that suits your marketing and PR budget. Use our unique advertising features to ensure your event is seen by your target market. Narrow your audience by interest, what they like to talk about, what event types they have been to and what they have tickets for already. This list is by no means exhaustive and we would love to have your input.

Entertain

Sell tickets with ease and have all payment and commission systems taken care of by us. Use traditional NFC (near field communication) readers or ticket check with Android tablets at the door. Ensure only the devices you allow to be used are actually used at checking time to prevent ticket fraud. When all is done, browse our event report to see comments on what users thought about it so as to improve future events.

Get Paid!

On completion of the event, have the money directly transferred to your bank account. If you wish to avoid nasty bank fees we can deal in Paypal or Stripe account systems to facilitate your needs. Anything else that would make your life easier? Please call and we'll help!
